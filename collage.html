ABOUT US

Buoyed by the unstinting support extended by the public to Christ Nagar Group of Institutions, Christ Nagar College, affiliated to University of Kerala, Thiruvananthapuram, was established by CMI Fathers, the pioneers in the field of education and social reforms in the year 2012 at Maranalloor, a sleepy hamlet in Thiruvananthapuram District just 12 kms away from the city limits. It is a premier institution set on a sprawling campus of 37.5 acres of land enveloped by the bounty of nature. The salubrious and silent surroundings of the college provide a conducive atmosphere for the teaching-learning process. The diligence and dedication of CMI Fathers and the conscientiousness and commitment of the faculty members and the elan and ebullience of the students enabled it to carve a niche for itself within a short while.

vision

To be a National leader in transforming lives through an excellent, innovative, radical, rigorous, relevant and compassionate approach to education.
